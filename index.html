How It Works

A new kind of agency.
Not a DIY kit.

Propel RE remains your licensed agent of record. You take on the buyer-facing parts of the campaign. We provide the technology, the processes, and expert guidance behind every interaction.

1
You meet your Propel RE agent
A full strategy session covering pricing, comparable sales, campaign structure, and exactly what to say and what to avoid throughout your sale. You leave knowing the plan inside out.
2
We launch your campaign professionally
Photography, floor plans, listing copy and portal submission on realestate.com.au and domain.com.au — all managed by Propel RE. Your listing goes live as an owner-occupier campaign, fully backed by a licensed agency.
3
You run open homes. Your agent handles the rest.
You open the door and welcome buyers in. The platform logs every visitor automatically via the check-in app. All buyer communication outside the inspection is handled by your licensed agent.
4
We manage every offer and negotiation
All offers flow through our structured platform. Your Propel RE agent handles all negotiation, counter-offers and price discussions. You review — we execute.
5
Contracts and settlement fully handled
Once the right offer is accepted, Propel RE manages all contracts, documentation and transaction management through to settlement.

Buyers get to meet the people who actually lived in the home. That's something no traditional agency model can replicate and it often builds a connection that translates into stronger, more committed offers.

Owner's Guide

Know exactly
what to say.

Before your campaign goes live, you'll complete a simple owner's guide with your Propel RE agent. It covers what you can do, what to hand to your agent, and how to handle common buyer questions with confidence.

✦ Your role as owner
✓Host open homes and give buyers access to the property
✓Welcome buyers warmly and walk them through at their pace
✓Register buyers on arrival using the check-in app
✓Answer factual questions about the property, features, renovations, the street
✓Direct everything else to your agent price, offers, next steps
○ Your agent handles everything else
→All buyer enquiries and follow-up outside the inspection
→Price, offers and negotiation of any kind
→Whether there are other offers on the table
→Contract terms, settlement dates and conditions
→All negotiations from first offer through to exchange
💬
The most useful sentence at any open home
"That's a great question our agent handles all of that and would love to chat with you. I'll make sure they follow up today." Simple, professional, and it works every time. Our platform gives you suggested responses for the most common buyer questions so you're never caught off guard.
Is It Right For You?

Built for confident sellers.
Not everyone.

An honest word before you decide

Selling your home is intense. There's a reason real estate agents have jobs it's a skilled, time-consuming process that involves documentation, negotiation, buyer management and a lot of emotional labour. If you'd rather someone else handle all of it, a traditional agent is absolutely the right choice.

Propel RE suits sellers who genuinely want to be involved who get energy from the process rather than anxiety, who have time to show their home on weekends, and who want to understand every step of their sale rather than just receive the result. If that's you, we think you'll find this model not just more cost-effective but genuinely more satisfying.

✦ A strong fit if…
✓You're comfortable welcoming buyers into your home on weekends
✓You're happy for your agent to handle all communication and negotiation
✓You want visibility into your campaign without managing every detail
✓You trust the process and want professionals handling the hard parts
✓You're selling a residential property in South East Queensland
○ Probably not the right fit if…
–You'd prefer a fully hands-off experience from start to finish
–Weekend availability is limited or hard to predict
–The property has significant complexity estate, dispute or commercial
–You find buyer-facing conversations stressful rather than energising
–You want your agent to handle every conversation end to end
