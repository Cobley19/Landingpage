Before anything else

Real estate agents exist for good reason.

Selling your home is one of the biggest financial decisions you will make. There are excellent agents out there and for many sellers, having a professional handle everything is absolutely the right call. Propel RE is a different choice, not a better one for everyone. If you want to be in the room rather than waiting by the phone, keep reading. If not, we will tell you that honestly in our first conversation.

Propel RE suits sellers who are comfortable opening their home to buyers on weekends and happy to let their agent handle everything else. Your role is straightforward. The platform and your agent do the heavy lifting.

Owner's Guide

Know exactly
what to say.

Before your campaign goes live, you will complete a simple owner's guide with your Propel RE agent covering what you can do, what to hand to your agent, and how to handle common buyer questions with confidence.

✦ Your role as owner
✓Host open homes and give buyers access to the property
✓Welcome buyers warmly and walk them through at their pace
✓Register buyers on arrival using the check-in app
✓Answer factual questions about the property, features, renovations, the street
✓Refer price and offer questions to your agent
○ Your agent handles
→All buyer enquiries and follow-up outside the inspection
→Price, offers and negotiation of any kind
→Whether there are other offers on the table
→Contract terms, settlement dates and conditions
→Everything from first offer through to exchange
